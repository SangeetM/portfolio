About Me

I'm a Data Scientist

I started my journey in this world of Intelligent Systems 2 years with an inclination towards research related to real time streaming data, owing to my experience with using Machine Learning for Financial Market. Shortly after graduating from SOA University, I joined the Innovation & Development Labs team at Mu Sigma Inc. where I work on a wide variety of interesting and meaningful data science projects on a daily basis.


I strong believe in the motto of "Learning over Knowing" and the benefits of sharing my research with the AI/ML community which lead to building this website so that I can share my research with fellow researcher and help them in their journey towards bringing a change in the world.
